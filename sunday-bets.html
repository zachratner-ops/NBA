NFL Sunday Bets 🏈
One person is the house and throws out a slate of 5 bets. Take any odd number — 1, 3, or 5 — at $20 each, you get the side as written. Take all 5 and there's a $20 kicker to whoever wins the majority.
📖 Full rules
🏠 Each week one member is the house and posts 5 bets
💵 Every bet is $20, head-to-head against the house — you get the side as written, the house has the fade
🔢 You must take an odd number of bets: 1, 3, or 5 (no ties with the house)
🎰 Take all 5 → $20 kicker to whoever wins the majority of the graded bets
🤝 A push is no action: $0 both ways and it doesn't count toward the majority — if pushes leave the kicker tied, the kicker is void
