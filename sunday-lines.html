BBQC Sunday Lines 🔥
BBQC posts 5 lines. Everyone picks a side on 1, 3, or 5 of them — blind. Opposite sides pair off at $10 a head, a press doubles your stakes on one pick, and a 5-for-5 sweep taxes the whole table.
📖 Full rules
🏠 BBQC posts the 5 lines each week (standard public lines) — they set the slate, they're not bets themselves
🎯 Each participant independently picks a side on 1, 3, or 5 of the lines
👀 Picks are blind — revealed simultaneously when the board locks
💵 Each line is a flat $10 head-to-head between every pair of people on opposite sides
🔥 Press one pick per week (optional, chosen blind): your matchups on that line go to $20 — $40 if both sides pressed
🎰 Sweep bonus: go 5-for-5 and collect $10 from every other participant who bet that week
🤝 Pushes = no action, $0 — and a push knocks you out of sweep contention
💰 Everything settles through the season ledger
